About Us

Sri Manakula Vinayaga Educational Trust was founded to provide quality and affordable education to the weaker sections of society and is one of the best engineering college in Pondicherry. The trust established Sri Manakula Vinayagar Engineering College (SMVEC) in 1999. SMVEC is an autonomous institution affiliated to Pondicherry University.

SMVEC has a sprawling green campus with well-furnished infrastructure. The college has a Wi-Fi enabled campus, central library, high-end computing facilities, individualized department buildings with advanced laboratories, centralized air-conditioned auditorium, campus security cameras and video surveillance system, mineral water plant, hostels for both boys and girls, and other essential amenities like ATM and post office.SMVEC has been accredited by NAAC and NBA. The college has a good placement record with students getting job offers from top companies in India and abroad. SMVEC students have won many awards and accolades for their academic achievements.

The trust established Sri Manakula Vinayagar Engineering College (SMVEC) in 1999. SMVEC is an autonomous institution affiliated to Pondicherry University. It offers a variety of undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs in Engineering, Arts and Science, Allied Health sciences, School of Agriculture, Centre of Legal Education, School of Physiotherapy and School of Pharmacy.

Sri Manakula Vinayaga Educational Trust was founded to provide quality technical, medical, and other relevant education, especially to the disadvantaged members of society.The trust provides state-of-the-art facilities and modern technologies to its colleges to help students become employable and successful entrepreneurs. They also maintain discipline among the students and personally take care of their studies and progress. The trust believes that students are their customers and they take utmost care about their future.All the colleges of the trust have maintained high standards of discipline and have earned the respect and trust of the public and industrialists. This is the key to their success and has made them a leader in the education sector.
